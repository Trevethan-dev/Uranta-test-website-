Our Story

Born from the mat,
made for life.

Uranta was born from a simple belief: the clothes you wear during practice should carry the same intention as the practice itself. Purposeful, grounded, and beautifully made.

We work with ethical artisans and sustainable materials to create pieces that feel as good as they look — whether you are moving through a flow or moving through the world.
