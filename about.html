Our Philosophy

Comfortable, creative,
made with care.

At Uranta, we create clothing for a lifestyle. For people who celebrate their lives through the inspiration that movement gives — who like to dance, stretch, sweat, jump, and applaud every moment.

Yoga, work, the market, the city, the countryside. Uranta is designed to accompany you in your daily life and to promote a lifestyle that is healthy, aware, and committed to the community.

"We promote a healthy lifestyle, committed to the community and the environment we all share."

We Care for the Earth

Upcycling as a
way of life.

Dead Stock Fabrics

We work with fabrics no longer used on the cutting tables of large clothing companies — materials that would otherwise be wasted. We give them a new life. This is upcycling: transforming discarded materials into products of greater quality and environmental value.

Less Than 3% Waste

Our leftover fabric is less than 3% — used for bags and labels. The best remnants are donated to IMMI, who work with people living with cancer, sewing beautiful turbans. Nothing is wasted. Everything has a purpose.
